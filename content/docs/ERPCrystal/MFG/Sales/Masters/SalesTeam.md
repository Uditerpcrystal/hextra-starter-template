## Introduction

This feature helps to organize Sales Team under a group head.

#### Mandatory Fields

|Mandatory Fields|  
  |:------
  | Group No <br> - Auto generated by System
  | Group Name 
  | Select Group Head <br> - Pre Selected (Options as per Sales officer master where status is Active).

#### Features

|Features|  
  |:------
  | Create New 
  | Delete
